Our Story

Founded in 2015, PetConnect began with a simple mission: to create connections between loving families and pets in need of forever homes.

Our Mission Meet Our Team

Our Mission & Vision

At PetConnect, we believe every pet deserves a loving home and every family deserves the joy of pet companionship. Our mission is to bridge the gap between homeless pets and caring adopters through responsible matching, education, and ongoing support.

Saving Lives, One Pet at a Time

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at PetConnect

Compassion

We approach every animal and every adopter with empathy, understanding, and kindness.

Responsibility

We take our role in animal welfare seriously, ensuring every adoption is the right match.

Community

We foster connections between pet lovers and work collaboratively with local shelters.

Education

We provide resources to help adopters become informed, responsible pet owners.

Quality Care

We ensure all pets receive proper medical attention, nutrition, and enrichment.

Lifelong Support

Our commitment continues after adoption with training resources and veterinary partnerships.
